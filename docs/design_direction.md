# Lumina OH — Design Direction

The design must reflect a calm spiritual atmosphere.

This product targets women interested in emotional healing and self-reflection.

---

# Target Audience

Japanese women  
Age 25 – 45

Interests:

- spirituality
- mindfulness
- healing
- emotional reflection
- beauty and lifestyle

---

# Emotional Tone

The interface should feel:

quiet  
soft  
spiritual  
elegant  
introspective  

The experience should slow the user down.

---

# Design Characteristics

Large white space  
Soft gradients  
Thin typography  
Subtle animation  

Avoid heavy UI elements.

Avoid corporate SaaS appearance.

---

# Visual Inspiration

Japanese spa websites  
Meditation apps  
Luxury skincare brands

The interface should feel closer to a **digital sanctuary** than a typical web app.
